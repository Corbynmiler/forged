The journal

Reflection that
actually sticks.

🏋️
Gym Mar 21

What felt strong today? What needs work?

Hit 80kg for 3 on bench. Shoulders felt tight — probably skipping warmup again. Need to be honest about that. The energy was there, form is slipping.

Quick note: Upper body · PR attempt
🥗
Eat better Mar 21

What did you actually eat today?

Meal prepped Sunday and it showed. Three full meals, actually tasted good. The prep makes the whole week easier — why don't I do this every weekend?

Prompts specific to each habit

Not "how did it go?" — but "what made today's session feel hard or easy?" Set your own prompts when you create a habit.

Quick notes + deep reflections

140-character quick note for context. Full journal entry when you have something real to say. Both show up in your journal history.

Past reflections surfaced in context

When you open a reflection, you see what you wrote last time. Patterns start becoming obvious fast.

Filterable by habit

The journal tab shows everything in one place. Filter to a single habit and read your own progression like a story.
